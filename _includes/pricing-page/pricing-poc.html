Unlock the benefits of OpenFaaS Pro today

If you're early on your journey, you can get a feel for what OpenFaaS is like by installing the Community Edition (CE). It's free to use, you can start building a Proof of Concept (PoC) with functions within a few minutes.

Whenever you'd like to move ahead with OpenFaaS for production, then you can set up a call with our team to learn more about OpenFaaS Pro and Enterprise.

Each Kubernetes cluster will need to be licensed and classed as either "production" or "staging". A production cluster serves traffic to users whether they are internal or external customers/processes. Any cluster used for shared testing and collaboration, is classed as "staging" and will be discounted.
